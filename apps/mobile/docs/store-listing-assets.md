### Long Description Draft (cross-platform)

> **Reclaim your attention. Ship what matters.**
>
> Unscroller replaces doomscrolling with creator mode. Launch Instagram™, TikTok™, X™ and more directly into productive surfaces while distracting feeds stay filtered out. Track your scroll-free streak, celebrate wins, and keep your focus on the work that matters.
>
> **What’s inside**
> • Focused provider launchers – open DMs, subscriptions, analytics, not endless feeds.  
> • AI-assisted accountability – quick prompts and journaling inside a calm workspace.  
> • Screen Time shields – block native apps in one tap with guided instructions.  
> • Insightful metrics – time saved, streaks, XP, trophies that celebrate real progress.  
> • Emergency calm – panic button, meditation, and breathing tools when overwhelm hits.  
>
> **Creator Mode subscription**  
> • $11.11/month, auto-renews until cancelled.  
> • Manage or cancel anytime under Settings → Account Management (in-app) or device subscriptions dashboard.  
> • Restore purchases via Settings on any supported device.  
>
> **Privacy & Safety**  
> • We filter content only inside Unscroller’s WebView. Other browsers remain untouched.  
> • Data is encrypted and deletable. Remove everything from Settings → Delete Account or visit our support portal.  
> • Community features include reporting, blocking, and active moderation.  
>
> Support: support@unscroller.app | https://unscroller.app/support

### Capture Tips
- Use simulator/device at 1284×2778 (iPhone Pro Max) and 1440×3200 (Pixel) for clean assets.  
- Hide debug UI (no redboxes/log overlays).  
- Ensure user data is anonymized or fictional.

## 3. Iconography & Branding

- Maintain monochrome provider icons (Apple guidelines).  
- Include new glyphs for “Create,” “Focus,” and “Shield” actions.  
- Export at all required resolutions: 1024×1024 (App Store), 512×512 (Play), plus rounded mask previews.  
- Background: deep navy gradient (#0D0D1A → #211C3F) with subtle nebula flare.

## 4. Legal Copy Checklist

- **Subscription terms**: Auto-renews, cancellation instructions, restore instructions.  
- **Privacy disclaimers**: Filtering limited to Unscroller WebView, data deletion options, analytics usage.  
- **Third-party trademarks**: Instagram™, TikTok™, Facebook™, YouTube™, X™ – include “not affiliated” clause.  
- **Support statement**: Provide email + URL consistently across stores and in-app.
