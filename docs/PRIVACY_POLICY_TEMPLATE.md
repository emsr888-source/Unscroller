# Privacy Policy for Unscroller

**Last Updated:** November 7, 2025

## Introduction

Welcome to Unscroller ("we," "our," or "us"). We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, and share information about you when you use our mobile application.

## Information We Collect

### Information You Provide

- **Account Information:** Email address, name, username, profile picture
- **Profile Data:** Bio, current project, preferences
- **User Content:** Posts, comments, journal entries, todos, goals
- **Communication:** Messages sent to other users

### Automatically Collected Information

- **Usage Data:** How you interact with the app, features used, time spent
- **Device Information:** Device type, operating system, unique device identifiers
- **Log Data:** IP address, browser type, pages visited, time and date of visits

### Information from Third Parties

- **Authentication Providers:** If you sign in with Apple, Google, or other services
- **Payment Information:** Processed securely through RevenueCat and Apple/Google

## How We Use Your Information

We use collected information to:

- **Provide and improve our services**
- **Personalize your experience**
- **Track your progress** (streaks, achievements, goals)
- **Enable social features** (community posts, friend connections)
- **Send notifications** about achievements, messages, or app updates
- **Analyze and improve** app performance
- **Prevent fraud and abuse**
- **Comply with legal obligations**

## How We Share Your Information

### Information Shared Publicly

- **Profile information** (name, username, avatar, bio)
- **Community posts** and comments
- **Achievements** and streaks (visible to friends/community)

### Information Shared with Service Providers

- **Supabase:** Database hosting and authentication
- **RevenueCat:** In-app purchase management
- **Analytics providers:** For app improvement (if you opt in)

### We DO NOT:

- ❌ Sell your personal information
- ❌ Share your data with advertisers
- ❌ Use your data for targeted advertising
- ❌ Access your private messages or journal entries without permission

## Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your data, including:

- **Encryption** of data in transit and at rest
- **Row Level Security** for multi-tenant data isolation
- **Secure authentication** through industry-standard protocols
- **Regular security audits** and updates

However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security.

## Your Rights

You have the right to:

- **Access** your personal data
- **Correct** inaccurate data
- **Delete** your account and data
- **Export** your data
- **Opt-out** of non-essential communications
- **Withdraw consent** at any time

### How to Exercise Your Rights

- **In-app:** Settings → Account → Privacy
- **Email:** privacy@unscroller.app
- **Delete account:** Settings → Account → Delete Account

## Data Retention

We retain your information as long as:

- Your account is active
- Necessary to provide services
- Required by law

When you delete your account:
- Personal data is deleted within 30 days
- Some data may be retained for legal/safety reasons
- Community posts may remain anonymized

## Children's Privacy

Unscroller is not intended for users under 13 (or 16 in Europe). We do not knowingly collect data from children. If you believe we have collected data from a child, contact us immediately.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy occasionally. We will notify you of significant changes via:

- In-app notification
- Email notification
- Updated "Last Updated" date

Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance.

## International Data Transfers

Your information may be transferred to and processed in countries other than your own. We ensure appropriate safeguards are in place.

## European Privacy Rights (GDPR)

EU/EEA residents have additional rights under GDPR:

- Right to data portability
- Right to restriction of processing

- Right to lodge a complaint with supervisory authority
